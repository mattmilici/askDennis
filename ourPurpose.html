Our Purpose

Dennis was created as a fun way to say no... most of the time! He is known to occassionally say yes when he is feeling generous.

Those complex algorithms Dennis uses are really just a 5% chance of saying yes. So whether you're kids are begging for new toys or your best friend wants you to buy the next round of beers, don't worry, because Dennis will take the stress off you and be the bad guy!
